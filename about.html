Hola, I'm Traveling Bear Memo.

My actual name is Guillermo but I go by Memo. For the past few years I've been working multiple jobs, my profession was Security. I never had the opportunity to finish college. But that never held me back from achieving my life long dream of traveling the world. I have traveled close to half of the United States. In addition I have traveled to 10 countries (including the United States) and counting; and also 4 continents. Reason I created this website was to help people that have a degree/no degree, career/no career, multiple jobs, kids/no kids, work/unemployed; or any other responsibilities to have an opportunity to travel. The goal is to help anyone accomplish great wonders.
