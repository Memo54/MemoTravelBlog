Skyscanner

-Best website for cheap flights and multi-city flights
Kayak

-Best website for cheap flights and multi-city flights
Hitlist

-Best website for cheap flights
Hopper

-Best website for cheap flights
AirBnb

-Best website to book to stay for the night: Shared Rooms, Private Rooms, Apartments, and Houses (Avg. price is $40-$65 per night)
HostelWorld

-Best website to book Bunk beds in Shared Dorms, and Private Rooms (Avg. price is $10-$45 per night)
CouchSurfing

-Best website for requesting to sleep on a host's couch either for free
InsureMyTrip

-Best website for staying safe overseas on a trip and you're covered for medical, injury, emergency; etc.
Tripadvisor

-Best website to inform yourself of the destination you're heading like price of food, transportation, local restaurants, pharmacy, police station; etc.
Nomadic Matt

-One website i recommend everyone should checkout, Nomadic Matt has years of experience on travel tips and advice
YouTube

-When it comes down to searching for more information. This is the Greatest Website to look up videos on travel knowledge, inspiration, intimate travel experiences, travel advice; or travel tips.
